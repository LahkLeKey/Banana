# ML Brain Domain Contract

**Feature**: 009-ml-brain-domain-spike  
**Date**: 2026-04-26

## Scope

Defines planning contract for left/right/full brain domain framing and model-family mapping.

## Domain Contract

1. Left Brain is the estimation/calibration domain and anchors Regression family ownership.
2. Right Brain is the categorical threshold domain and anchors Binary family ownership.
3. Full Brain is the contextual synthesis domain and anchors Transformer family ownership.
4. Domain definitions must include explicit boundary conditions and known risks.

## Mapping Contract

1. Each model family must have one primary domain mapping.
2. Secondary cross-domain interactions may be documented but cannot replace the primary mapping.
3. Mapping rationale must be evidence-backed by matrix dimensions.

## SPIKE Output Contract

1. Comparison matrix must score all three families with shared normalized dimensions.
2. At least one readiness packet per domain must be produced.
3. Each readiness packet must define validation-lane expectations and contract risks.

## Validation Contract

Minimum closure evidence for this SPIKE:

- Domain definitions complete and non-overlapping
- Mapping matrix complete for Regression/Binary/Transformer
- Readiness packet set complete for Left/Right/Full domains
