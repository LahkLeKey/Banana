# Readiness Packet Quality Checklist (US3)

**Date**: 2026-04-26
**Purpose**: Gate to confirm all three domain readiness packets are complete and implementation-ready.

## Checklist

### Packet Completeness

- [ ] Left Brain readiness packet exists in readiness-packet-left-brain.md
- [ ] Right Brain readiness packet exists in readiness-packet-right-brain.md
- [ ] Full Brain readiness packet exists in readiness-packet-full-brain.md
- [ ] All three packets are referenced in consolidated readiness-packets.md

### Per-Packet Content

For each packet (Left, Right, Full):

- [ ] Objective is clearly stated and bounded
- [ ] Scope boundary explicitly states what is in and out of scope for the follow-up slice
- [ ] Validation lane expectations are defined (which lanes run, what assertions are required)
- [ ] Risk items from domain-risk-register.md are referenced
- [ ] Any planned-grade scores from uncertainty-impact-notes.md are referenced
- [ ] Follow-up slice is bounded enough to begin without a rediscovery meeting

### Consistency

- [ ] All packets reference terminology from terminology-rules.md
- [ ] All packets are consistent with brain-domain-definitions.md and model-family-mappings.md
- [ ] No packet extends scope beyond what the SPIKE supports (no production behavior change recommendations)
